Mr. Calm is one of our counselors that will meet with you one on one as well as in a group setting twice a week. Mr. Calm is also one of our meditaion teachers who will help guide you through the meditation process.

Miss Energy will guide you through aerobic exercises, water-activities, yoga & other exercises to help you learn to take better care of your self.

Miss Brave will help you with Equine and small animal therapy and will guide you through the process of putting your trust in the animals and teach you how to properly take care of them as well as your self. Miss Brave will also take those who would like out on weekend campouts so that you can learn to trust and enjoy yourself in the outdoors.

Little Miss Who Needs a Friend when you have a cat is our other Equine and small animal therapist and will guide you through the process of putting your trust in the animals and teach you how to properly take care of them as well as your self. She also takes time a few times a week to help parents to learn positive parenting techniques, coping skills and grounding to help not only the parent/s but also the children learn how to deal with their PTSD, anxiety and other issues going on at this difficult time in their lives.

Miss Giggles is one of our Recreational Therapists that comes in three times a week to help you learn new skills to handle your mental as well as your physical health issues. She also helps out in the group sessions with Mr. Calm twice a week.

Miss Helpful is a counselor here at Cape Hope that will meet with you individually. She also helps in small group settings on how to learn to set boundaries, what self care and self love are and how to incorporate them into your life. You can usually find Miss Helpful in the kitchen not only cooking main meal items but also her favorite desserts and teaching anyone willing to learn new recipes that are quick, easy, nutritous and delicious

Miss Kind is our water counselor. She does all kinds of water activities and it seems like she lives in the lake as you hardly see her on dry ground. She will guide you through water exercises, take you out canoeing, rafting, fishing or whatever else she comes up with.

Miss Magic is a Physical Therapist that comes in three to five times a week to help those that need to work the kinks out of their bodies. She also works with parent/s on how to keep your room/home clean and organized.

Miss Sunshine is a wellness coach that comes in to work with our parent/s to improve your health and well-being and helps you to set up some health habits to keep on track for your future.

Miss Yes is our life skills coach that will help you with deciding a career path, whether or not to go back to school as well as setting & achieving goals and personal growth.

Mr. Happy is our Occupational Therapist that comes in two to three times a week to help our parents to learn the skills needed for daily living and working. He also will work with you in the gym and teach you activities to keep healthy for your lifetime.

Mr. Perfect is our job skills coach and will also help you with your resume and cover letters. If you need more skills then he will work with you to either find classes you can take or another program to help you get the position you would like.
